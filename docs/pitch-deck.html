HospitAI

The Problem

Hotels run on fragmented systems with zero cross-system intelligence

  • A typical hotel uses 8-12 separate software systems that don't communicate
  • When a guest checks out, housekeeping doesn't know automatically
  • When occupancy drops, energy systems keep running at full capacity
  • Revenue decisions are made on gut feeling, not data
  • Staff spend hours on manual coordination

The result:

  • Higher operational costs
  • Lower revenue capture
  • Worse guest experience
  • Unnecessary energy waste

The gap is widest in aparthotels — the fastest-growing segment with the lowest tech adoption.
