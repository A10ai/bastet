# HospitAI — Executive Summary

**AI-Powered Hotel Operating System | UK-Registered Company | Seeking Academic and Grant Partnership**

---

## What is HospitAI?

HospitAI is an AI-native operating system for hotels that connects six core operational systems — property management, revenue, housekeeping, maintenance, energy, and guest intelligence — through a single artificial intelligence layer. Unlike incumbent solutions that bolt AI onto legacy software, HospitAI was built from the ground up around cross-system intelligence, enabling automated decision-making across the entire hotel operation.

A working prototype is live at **app.hospitai.uk**. The platform is not conceptual — it is built, deployed, and configured for a real 270-unit property.

## The Opportunity

The global hospitality technology market is projected to reach **$220 billion by 2030**, yet hospitality has one of the lowest AI adoption rates among major industries. The aparthotel and extended-stay segment — the fastest-growing accommodation category — has particularly low technology adoption and is underserved by existing solutions designed for traditional hotel chains.

Egypt, our first deployment market, welcomed **15.8 million visitors in 2024** with a government target of **30 million by 2028-2030**, driving substantial new hotel construction along the Red Sea coast.

## What Has Been Built

The platform includes a full property management system (270 apartments configured), an AI Command Centre with health scoring and occupancy forecasting, a Revenue Copilot with dynamic pricing and what-if simulation, five Smart Automations (pricing, housekeeping, maintenance, VIP preparation, energy standby), a cross-system Event Bus processing 13 event types through 7 active handlers, an Energy Dashboard with waste detection and CO2 tracking, Guest Intelligence with lifetime value scoring and churn detection, an AI Chat Assistant for natural language queries, and comprehensive reporting with CSV export. The technology stack comprises Next.js, Supabase (44 database tables), Claude API, and Vercel cloud hosting.

## Pilot Property

**Bastet Hurghada** — a 270-unit aparthotel (180 studios, 76 one-bed, 10 two-bed, 4 penthouses) in the Kawthar District, Hurghada, Egypt. The property is approximately 32,000 sqm across 6 floors in a single building, opening Q2 2027. Phase 1 will deploy approximately 100 units, with Phase 2 bringing the full property online by Q4 2027.

This greenfield deployment provides an ideal controlled environment for measuring AI impact on energy consumption, operational efficiency, and revenue performance — producing verifiable, publishable results.

## R&D Collaboration Areas

We have identified six specific research areas suitable for academic partnership through the Knowledge Transfer Partnership (KTP) programme:

1. **Reinforcement learning for dynamic pricing optimisation** — moving from rule-based to ML-driven revenue management
2. **NLP and LLM systems for multilingual guest communication** — Arabic, Russian, German, and other key languages
3. **Computer vision for occupancy analytics and security** — real-world CV deployment in a hospitality setting
4. **IoT sensor networks for predictive maintenance** — sensor-to-AI integration for building management
5. **Energy optimisation models using occupancy prediction** — measurable CO2 reduction through intelligent energy management
6. **Recommendation systems for guest personalisation** — deployed recommender using live guest data

Each area offers publishable academic output, access to a real deployment environment generating live operational data, and the opportunity for MSc or PhD student placements with direct industry impact.
